About IIMM Ayodhya

The Indian Institute of Materials Management (IIMM) has approved a new branch in Ayodhya, the sacred city of Lord Ram, to support the "Ek Bharat Shresth Bharat" initiative.

The inauguration will be an illustrious event featuring prominent figures from the healthcare industry, academic institutions, hotel and hospitality sectors, and other industries, along with IIMM dignitaries. This initiative aims to enhance the nation's growth and development.

The event promises to be memorable and impactful, highlighting the importance of collaboration across various sectors. Learners, researchers, scholars, and professionals interested in contributing to nation-building are invited to engage with this new branch.

For more information and to get involved, individuals can visit: www.iimm.org

Target Group

Working professionals in the field of:

  • Stores & Purchase
  • Supply Chain Management (SCM)
  • Finance & Accounts
  • Logistics
  • Materials Management

Also suitable for students pursuing UG/PG degrees or diploma programs.
